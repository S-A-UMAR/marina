About Marina Gadgets Kano

We are on a mission to make premium technology honest, accessible, and trustworthy in northern Nigeria.

Our Story

Marina Gadgets Kano was founded on one simple belief: every person in Kano deserves access to genuine, tested, and quality technology — without having to worry about being deceived.

Starting from the streets of Kano, we built our reputation on transparency. Every UK Used device we sell is subjected to a rigorous diagnostic and testing process. Every shipment is photographed and sealed.

Today, Marina Gadgets serves thousands of satisfied customers across Kano and Nigeria — and we're just getting started.
