Shipping & Delivery

Fast, safe, and transparent delivery across Nigeria.

Same-Day Kano Delivery

Because we are based right here in Kano, we pride ourselves on exceptional local delivery speed.

  • Orders placed before 3:00 PM are delivered the exact same day.
  • Orders placed after 3:00 PM are delivered the next morning.
  • You can opt to pick up your order directly from our store by choosing "Request a Call" at checkout.

Cost: Standard Kano delivery is ₦{{ site_settings.default_shipping_fee|floatformat:0 }} (Free for orders over ₦{{ site_settings.free_shipping_threshold|floatformat:0 }}).

Nationwide Shipping

We ship to all 36 states in Nigeria using trusted logistics partners (e.g., GIG Logistics, DHL).

  • Abuja & Kaduna: Usually arrives within 1-2 business days.
  • Lagos & South-West: Usually arrives within 2-3 business days.
  • South-East & South-South: Usually arrives within 3-4 business days.

Package Security: Every device sent outside Kano is photographed, packed securely with bubble wrap, and sealed with tamper-proof tape before being handed over to the courier. We will send you the tracking number as soon as it ships.
